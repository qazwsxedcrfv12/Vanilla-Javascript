Maximum Cost Bits

You are given an Array consisting of N positive integers where each integer is a power of 2. Initially, the score is 0. You 
can apply teh folowing operation until N>t;
  • Choose two elements x and y
  • Add the value x ^ y to the score
  • Remove x and y from A

Task

Determine the maximum score that ca be obtained after applying at most [ N/2 ] operations.

Notes

  • The array may contain duplicate elements.
  • ^ represents BITWISE XOR

Example:

Assumptions

  • N = 5
  • A = [1,2,64,64,32]

Approach

  • If you choose i=2 and j=4 after taking XOR of both elements at ith and jth positions, you will get 64^32 = 96. You add this value to the cost, now the array will become [1,2,64].
  • In the second operation, you choose i=1 and j=2, we will get 2^64 = 66, Cost becomes 96+66 = 162, the array becomes [1].
  • Now, you cannot do more operations. You can show that this is the maximum xost that you can get.
Hence the answer is 162
